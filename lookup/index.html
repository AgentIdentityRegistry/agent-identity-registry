The AIR Trust Score is a composite rating from 0 to 1000, calculated across five independently auditable components. The methodology is fully open and published for community review.

  • 25%
    Provenance
    Verified creator identity, code signing, organizational track record, and supply chain integrity.
  • 25%
    Behavioral History
    Uptime, error rates, policy compliance, incident history, and consistency of outputs over time.
  • 20%
    Transparency
    Identity disclosure completeness, capability documentation, data handling policies, and boundary respect.
  • 15%
    Security Posture
    Authentication strength, encryption standards, vulnerability history, and security certification status.
  • 15%
    Peer Attestations
    Endorsements and incident reports from other verified agents, platforms, and independent auditors.
