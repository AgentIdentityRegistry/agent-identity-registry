# School of AIR

**Internal curriculum for the AIR founding team.**
Read these in order. Each builds on the last. Everything is explained simply — no jargon without a definition.

## Curriculum

| # | Module | What You'll Learn | Time |
|---|--------|-------------------|------|
| 01 | [The Problem](01-the-problem.md) | Why AI agents need identity. The real-world scenarios that make this urgent. | 10 min |
| 02 | [What AIR Is](02-what-air-is.md) | What we built, how it works, what each piece does. The 2-minute pitch. | 15 min |
| 03 | [How Trust Scores Work](03-trust-scores.md) | The 5-component model, how scores are calculated, what the grades mean. | 10 min |
| 04 | [The Standards World](04-standards-world.md) | W3C, IETF, DIF, NIST — who they are, what they do, why they matter to us. | 15 min |
| 05 | [Building Blocks: DIDs and VCs](05-dids-and-vcs.md) | The two W3C standards AIR is built on. How they work. Why they exist. | 15 min |
| 06 | [The Content Provenance Story](06-content-provenance.md) | C2PA, CAWG, and why "who made this content?" is a billion-dollar question. | 15 min |
| 07 | [Where AIR Fits](07-where-air-fits.md) | How AIR connects to every major initiative. Our role in the ecosystem. | 10 min |
| 08 | [What We Have vs. What We Claim](08-honest-assessment.md) | Brutal honesty about our current state. What works, what doesn't, what's next. | 10 min |
| 09 | [Talking to People](09-talking-to-people.md) | How to explain AIR to different audiences. Cheat sheets for every scenario. | 15 min |

**Total: ~2 hours**

## How to Use This

1. Read modules 01-05 first — this is your foundation
2. Read 06-07 before any standards body conversation
3. Read 08-09 right before any call or meeting
4. Keep the cheat sheets from module 09 handy during conversations
