# Cover Email for NIST CAISI Late Comment Submission

**To:** AI-Identity@nist.gov
**Subject:** Public Comment — NCCoE Concept Paper on Software and AI Agent Identity and Authorization (Agent Identity Registry Foundation)

---

Dear NCCoE AI Agent Identity Project Team,

We respectfully submit the attached public comment in response to the NCCoE concept paper, "Accelerating the Adoption of Software and AI Agent Identity and Authorization." We recognize the formal comment period closed on April 2, 2026, and appreciate your consideration of this submission.

The Agent Identity Registry (AIR) Foundation is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit building neutral, open-standards infrastructure for AI agent identity and trust. Our comment addresses four areas we believe warrant additional attention in the concept paper: consumer-facing trust visibility, neutral governance of identity infrastructure, multi-dimensional trust scoring, and interoperability across the fragmented standards landscape.

Our attached comment includes:

1. Introduction to AIR and its mission
2. Response to specific concept paper sections, highlighting gaps AIR addresses
3. Our technical approach — built on W3C DIDs, Verifiable Credentials, and NIST-aligned cryptographic standards
4. Recommendations for neutral governance of agent identity infrastructure
5. References to our working specification and live demonstration

We also wish to express our interest in participating as a collaborator in any NCCoE demonstration project that emerges from this concept paper. Our working demo is available at https://agentidentityregistry.org/lookup, and our full specification is published at https://github.com/AgentIdentityRegistry/agent-identity-registry.

We welcome the opportunity to discuss our work with the project team at your convenience.
