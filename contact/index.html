Reach Out

Get in Touch

We're a two-person team building neutral identity infrastructure for AI agents. Real responses, sometimes slow.

Response time: We aim to reply within 5 business days. For urgent security disclosures, please flag "SECURITY" in the subject line. For everything else, expect a thoughtful reply rather than an immediate one.

By Topic

Seven dedicated addresses, each routing to the right inbox. Pick the most specific one — it reaches us faster.

About Our Replies

We're transparent about being small. Two people. Limited bandwidth.

What we will do: Read every message. Reply with real thought (not a templated response). Be honest about what we can and can't commit to.

What we might not do: Reply within a day. Schedule calls with everyone who asks (please consider whether email can solve it first). Promise timelines we can't keep.

If you don't hear back in two weeks, send a polite nudge — emails do occasionally slip past us. We genuinely want to hear from you.
